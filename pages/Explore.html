Types of Books..!


Here are some common types of books:

Fiction :

1. Novels: Book-length works of fiction, often with complex characters and plotlines. 2. Short Stories: Shorter works of fiction, often with a single plot or theme. 3. Science Fiction: Fiction that explores the impact of science and technology on society. 4. Fantasy: Fiction that involves magical or supernatural elements.

Non-Fiction :

1. Biographies: Books about the lives of real people. 2. Memoirs: Personal accounts of an individual's life experiences. 3. History: Books about historical events, periods, or figures. 4. Science: Books about scientific concepts, theories, and discoveries.

Other Genres :

1. Romance: Fiction that focuses on romantic relationships. 2. Horror: Fiction that aims to evoke fear, anxiety, or unease. 3. Thriller: Fiction that involves suspense, action, and often crime or danger. 4. Historical Fiction: Fiction set in a specific historical time period.
